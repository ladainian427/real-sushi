Creation

Unlike most other foods, sushi is often considered a form of art. There are many techniques to the creation of sushi as each chef has their own preferred method. The various tools and techniques used can greatly separate a high grade sushi roll to an amateur one. The creation of sushi is not something to be taken lightly, especially with the complexity of ingredients and rules needed to make each roll. It is because of these rules that make sushi not only aesthetically pleasing but tantalizing to the taste.
