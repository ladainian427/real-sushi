Creation

Unlike most other foods, sushi is often considered a form of art. There are many techniques to the creation of sushi as each chef has their own preferred method. The various tools and techniques used can greatly separate a high grade sushi roll to an amateur one. The creation of sushi is not something to be taken lightly, especially with the complexity of ingredients and rules needed to make each roll. It is because of these rules that make sushi not only aesthetically pleasing but tantalizing to the taste.

Advanced Techniques

Advanced techniques are use by those who take making sushi as a serious art. These techniques can either deal with making it aesthetically nice or techniques on rolling that maximize the sushi taste and structure.

Top Pages for Advanced Techniques

The following sites are advanced tutorials that want to try and roll sushi professionally.
Sushi Trainer | Advanced Tutorial

Sushi Trainer is an online sushi school that requires a fee to use. You may purchase a monthly subscription or a yearly subscription. Sushi Trainer has over 300 videos and a message board for learning the advanced and professional sushi making techniques. If you don't want to buy a subscription, they also have free sample movies!

I Make Sushi | Advanced Tutorial

I Make Sushi gives tutorials and guides more on the presentation on the sushi you have already prepared and how to prepare the condiments such as gari(ginger) and wasabi. They also show how the presentation and plates also affects making sushi at home.

Delica | Advanced Tutorial

For those of you in San Francisco, you're in luck. Delica, a Japanese delicatessen offers many classes on sushi, including their Master Sushi class which is offered in three parts and provides invaluable tips on making advanced sushi.
