Sushi Info

Coming from the country of Japan, many new foods and foreign phrases in sushi culture may confuse those who have no prior exposure to the cuisine. Even those who do often find themselves lost in the magnitude of fish names and jargon. With the right resources, navigating the world of sushi will become a much smoother journey.

Jargon and Sushi Vocabulary

Sushi culture is surrounded by jargon and vocabulary that can often confuse newcomers.

Top Pages for Sushi Jargon

The following are sites containing English and Japanese terms oftens used in sushi restaurants.

Sushi Encyclopedism | Glossary

This self-proclaimed "Ultimate Sushi Gallery" truly is what it claims to be. Containing both Japanese text, romanized Japanese, and English translations of pretty much anything relating to sushi. They even have categories at the top to jump to specific parts of the chart (fish, shellfish, etc.).

The Sushi Chronicles | Vocabulary

Although not the most comprehensive site, it contains useful terms and phrases to use in Japanese restaurant settings. Also includes the names of some types of sushi.

Asuka | Nigiri Names

This page has very clear pictures and names of common nigiri. Perfect for those who need a visual guide to what they order.

Sushi Fan | Glossary

Another good glossary page with the English pronunciations of many Japanese sushi-related jargon. Has good descriptions and includes some nice images of different sushi types along the right hand side of the page.
