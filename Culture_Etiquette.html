Culture

Originating from the culturally rich region of Japan, sushi is not only considered a delicacy for consuming, but with it comes history and traditions that must be followed strictly and still exist today. When consuming sushi, it is important to understand the culture that comes behind it to truly enjoy this fine dish.

The Etiquette of Eating Sushi

Eating sushi is unlike the consumption of any other meal. When talking about eating sushi, one must appreciate and take into account the incredible amount of culture and etiquette that must be followed. Culture in Japan is not something to be taken lightly and when eating sushi, these are must do’s and don’ts that one needs to be aware of.
