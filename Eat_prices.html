Eat

Sushi, and Japanese cuisine in general, may seem daunting to many newcomers. Though there is no right or wrong way to eat sushi, there are guidelines that define what is considered proper ettiquette. The wide variety of ingredients, forms, and cultural customs associated with the cuisine may be foreign to many people, and even those with previous knowledge might be misinformed about the specifics of traditional etiquette. Provided with the right preparations and information, one may find the world of sushi to be quite rewarding.

General Prices

Although there is no set market price for sashimi and nigiri due to the fact that a lot of it is seasonal, the following are menu's and places where you can shop for such items. This will provide a general overview of what to expect for specific sushi and nigiri.

Catalina Offshore Products | Prices

This is an online seafood import and export company. The sushi/sashimi grade seafood is all caught fresh and is used in a variety of different sushi bars. This site gives you a better idea of the different sushi/sashimi types and their respective prices.

AGBase | Prices

This site allows you to buy sashimi directly from their site and have it shipped to you. The site shows the prices of what you can buy to give an idea of generally which sashimi are the cheapest or most expensive.

Greggman | Toro Prices

Toro is the most expensive type of sashimi. This blog covers the which parts of the tuna are the most expensive and how to distinguish them.

Nobu | Menu

Nobu is an exquisite sushi restaurant. Their menu can give you a good idea of how sashimi is normally priced and which ones to expect to be pricer than the other ones.
