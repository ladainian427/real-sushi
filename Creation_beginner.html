Creation

Unlike most other foods, sushi is often considered a form of art. There are many techniques to the creation of sushi as each chef has their own preferred method. The various tools and techniques used can greatly separate a high grade sushi roll to an amateur one. The creation of sushi is not something to be taken lightly, especially with the complexity of ingredients and rules needed to make each roll. It is because of these rules that make sushi not only aesthetically pleasing but tantalizing to the taste.

Beginner Techniques

The following sites are tutorials for beginners that want to learn how to roll and make sushi. Although there is not one specific way to do it, there are basic rules that should be followed by anyone trying to attempt to roll sushi.

Top Pages for Beginner Techniques

Hub UK | Basic Tutorial

Hub UK shows you how to make a few different types of Sushi - from California Rolls to Tamago and Nigiri!
These guides are for the absolute beginner and tell you exactly how to prepare yourself to start making sushi!

Make My Sushi | Flash Tutorial

Makemysushi.com provides detailed guides with flash illustrations on how to make sushi for the first time! They also provide a few basic recipes and a webstore to get you started. Most of their guides are for rolled sushi(maki).

About | Video Tutorial

About.com gives us a lot of different links to videos on how to properly cut salmon, cucumber, avocado, and carrots as well as how to cook shrimp and rice for sushi. They also show us the techniques to make hand-pressed sushi..

Primer Magazine | Tutorial

The author of this article is a second generation Asian-American who thinks sushi should be made by anyone who enjoys eating it. She gives us a really basic tutorial on how to prepare and cut the ingredients and how to use the tools needed to make a few basic rolls.

Squidoo | Tutorial

Squidoo shows us what tools we need, how to prepare all the ingredients and the japanese names for everything. They tell us how to make sushi rice then go ahead and show us how to make a basic cucumber roll.
