Sushi Info

Coming from the country of Japan, many new foods and foreign phrases in sushi culture may confuse those who have no prior exposure to the cuisine. Even those who do often find themselves lost in the magnitude of fish names and jargon. With the right resources, navigating the world of sushi will become a much smoother journey.

Sushi Rolls and Dishes

When it comes to ordering sushi rolls, trying to determine what the roll consists of based only on the name is not entirely practical. Although most restaurants create their own special rolls, there are the common ones that are known to almost everyone, such as the California Roll or the Spicy Tuna Roll. Below is a comprehensive list of sites that provide the names of most rolls and the ingredients that you will find in them.

Top Pages for Sushi Rolls

The following are sites that list and describe different types of sushi rolls that you can find in most restaurants. THe following lists will provide a detailed explanation of ingredients and what the rolls consist of.

Food Warehouse | Rolls

This site provides a list of modern Japanese sushi rolls as well as western sushi rolls. Although it does not present many pictures, there is a good description of what each roll consists of.

Sushi Monsters | Rolls

Sushi monsters provides a list of general sushi rolls, noting that it is not close to the amount of rolls out there. However the list is a good guide for beginners. In addition to maki sushi rolls, they also have a list of individual maki types. The descriptions for each are detailed and informative.

Sushi Encyclopedia | Rolls

Sushi Encyclopedia provides a list of sushi roll names grouped by their styles. The two styles are fusion style and traditional style. The site provides a picture for each roll, a detailed description, as well as a list of ingredients.

Sushi Roll Recipe | Rolls

This is a very basic site focusing on the basic preparation and ingredients of the roll. In addition, there are also cook notes to show how the roll was made. This site is useful if you want to know how a particular roll was made.
