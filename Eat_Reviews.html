Eat

Sushi, and Japanese cuisine in general, may seem daunting to many newcomers. Though there is no right or wrong way to eat sushi, there are guidelines that define what is considered proper ettiquette. The wide variety of ingredients, forms, and cultural customs associated with the cuisine may be foreign to many people, and even those with previous knowledge might be misinformed about the specifics of traditional etiquette. Provided with the right preparations and information, one may find the world of sushi to be quite rewarding.

Restaurant Reviews

The following reviews covers local sushi eateries, finding the top sushi restaurants and bars around the country, and reviews of specific sushi rolls and ingredients.

Top Pages for Local Reviews

The following are restaurant finders to help you find a sushi restaurant closest to you. All of these sites provide user reviews and restaurant information to help your sushi experience a more convenient one.

Sushi Nomads | Reviews

This site has it all when reviewing sushi restaurants. It includes top reviewed by the site, top reviewed by the user, and the latest reviewed restaurants. When trying to find reviews for your local sushi restaurant this site works perfectly. The ratings for this site are categorized into atmosphere, service, nigiri, and rolls.

Yelp | Reviews

You can use Yelp to find your local sushi restaurant. Yelp gives you an overall rating by stars as well as user reviews and user comments. In addition, Yelp provides a price range, parking, accepts credit cards, has tv, waiter service, and much more. It also provides pictures.

Urbanspoon | Reviews

Urbanspoon is a quick way to find a restaurant nearest you. It is categorized into fine dining, higher priced, moderately priced, and cheap eats. In addition, it also has most recently reviewed and blogged restaurants specific to your area. The reviews they have come from critic reviews, diner reviews, blogger reviews, and even restaurant tweets. .

Zagat | Reviews

Although you need to sign up for Zagat, Zagat provides very specific and detailed reviews and information about your local sushi restaurant. The reviews cover sushi rank, food, d√©cor, service, cost, and rank in your specific location.

Sushi Monsters | Reviews

Sushimonsters covers a broad range of topics regarding sushi, one of which is restaurant reviews. There are not many reviews user reviews but the ones that do are useful. It also gives a special reviews section.
